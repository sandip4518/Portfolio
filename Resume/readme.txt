Color Palette
Primary Text Color:

Dark Navy Blue (#1A1A40): High contrast for readability while complementing the background.

Headings and Titles:

Deep Blue (#004080): Bold and professional for headings.

Accent Color (Buttons, Links):

Teal (#008080): Harmonizes with the background while standing out.

Section Backgrounds:

White (#FFFFFF): For sections requiring clean separation.

Light Gray (#F0F0F0): Subtle contrast for alternate sections.

Borders or Dividers:

Steel Blue (#4682B4): Soft yet noticeable for section dividers.

Call-to-Action Areas:

Bright Orange (#FF6600): Adds energy and draws attention to important elements like "Contact Me" buttons.

Footer Background:

Dark Gray (#333333): Professional and grounding for the footer.
